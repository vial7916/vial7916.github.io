welcome

Hi! My name is Victor Alamillo and I am a 20 year old Mexican-American from Fountain, CO.

I am a first-generation & low-income junior at the University of Colorado, Boulder - College of Media, Communication, and Information pursuing a B.S. in Strategic Communication with an emphasis in Public Relations and minoring in Creative Technology and Design.

On campus, I am a member of a few organizations, specifically: Alpha Kappa Psi - Gamma Zeta Chapter, CMCI Ambassadors, CU PRSSA, and CU QTPOC.

I currently hold two part-time positions on campus as well. I am the Marketing Director for CU GOLD, which is a leadership development organization. Additionally, I work at the Center for Academic Success and Engagement (CASE) building as a Student Assistant.

I hope this website will allow you to get to know me more, both professionally and personally. I hope you enjoy your time here!
